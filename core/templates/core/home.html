Who Is Ntatemoholo Seromo

Rooted in Ancestral Tradition

Ntatemoholo Seromo is a revered Sangoma healer with deep roots in Southern African spiritual tradition. A Sangoma is a traditional healer who acts as an intermediary between the living and the ancestral world — diagnosing illness, offering guidance, and facilitating healing ceremonies.

Through years of training under master healers, Ntatemoholo Seromo carries forward the sacred knowledge of bone throwing, herbal medicine, dream interpretation, and ancestral communication to bring balance and clarity to those in need.
